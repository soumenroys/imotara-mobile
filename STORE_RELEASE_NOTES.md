## Apple App Store — "What's New" (4000 chars max)

```
What's New in Imotara v1.0.2

We've made Imotara significantly more personal, multilingual, and insightful.

🌐 Full Indian Language Support
Emotion understanding now works across all 9 Indian languages — Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Malayalam, Gujarati, Punjabi, Odia, and Marathi. Whether you express yourself in your mother tongue or mix languages, Imotara understands you.

🧠 Companion Memory
Imotara now remembers context across your sessions. Your AI companion builds a gentle understanding of your journey over time, making each conversation feel more connected and personal.

📓 Mood Journal Export & Weekly Insights
Download your full reflection history anytime. Get a weekly emotional summary with trends so you can see how you've been feeling over time — not just today.

🎙 Voice Input
Speak your thoughts instead of typing them. Voice input is now available across the app.

💬 Smarter Conversation Starters
Multilingual starters help you begin a reflection even when you don't know where to start — available in your preferred language.

🔴 Crisis Support
A crisis card with multilingual support and a tap-to-call button for emergency contacts is now built into the app. Your safety matters.

🔥 Mood Streak & Daily Reminders
Stay consistent with your emotional check-ins. Track your mood streak and set a daily reminder to reflect.

🌬 Breathing Exercise
A simple guided breathing card is available whenever you need a moment to reset.

🌗 Dark & Light Mode
Full dark and light mode support, with theme-aware colors throughout every screen.

💬 Chat Improvements
React to messages, export your chat history, and enjoy a smooth 3-step onboarding experience the first time you launch.

☁️ Cloud Sync (Free for All)
Cloud sync is enabled and free for all users in this release. Your data stays with you across devices.

🐛 Bug Fixes
- Fixed history not loading in cloud mode
- Fixed session insights missing after sync
- Stability improvements across the app

---
Imotara is a private emotional companion. No ads. No data selling. Your reflections stay yours.
```
